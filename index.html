How RoleSync works

A one-time setup, then RoleSync quietly watches Stripe and keeps your Discord server perfectly in sync.

Step 1
Connect Stripe & Discord

You give RoleSync access to your Stripe account and add the RoleSync bot to your Discord server with permission to manage one role.

Step 2
Link product ‚Üí role

Choose the Stripe subscription price and the Discord role (for example: Paid Member). That link becomes the "truth" for RoleSync.

Step 3
Share your checkout link

Send your Stripe checkout link to your members. RoleSync handles the rest: assigning roles, removing them on cancellation, and keeping logs so you always know what happened.

What happens when someone cancels?
Stripe fires customer.subscription.deleted. RoleSync listens, finds the linked Discord user, and removes the paid role automatically.
Is this safe for my Discord server?
The bot only needs permission to manage one role. You keep full control over your server and can remove the bot at any time.
Can I stop using RoleSync?
Yes. You can cancel your RoleSync subscription in Stripe at any time. Your existing Discord roles won't be touched after that.
Do you plan to support more tiers?

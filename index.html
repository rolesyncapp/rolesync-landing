What happens when someone cancels?
Stripe sends a subscription update event. GuildPass listens, finds the linked Discord user, and removes the paid role automatically.
Do I need to give the bot admin?
No. GuildPass only needs permission to manage the roles you configure. You keep ownership of your server and can revoke access at any time.
Can I stop using GuildPass?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Existing Discord roles won't be touched after that.
Will there be more advanced tiers?
Yes. v2 will introduce multiple tiers (Bronze / Silver / Gold), advanced rules, and multi-server options. v1 users will be able to upgrade easily.
